We are a family-run coffee shop with a focus on providing a comfortable and relaxing space for the local community. We hope to serve as a "third place" for those who want to get out of the house or relax during their time away from work.

PERKS

You can enjoy . . .

  • B.Y.O.Mug 10% off drinks
  • KCRW Fringe Benefits Card 10% off order
  • FREE WI-FI
  • Plentiful indoor and outdoor seating
  • dog-friendly outdoor patio
  • “The Study” – a quiet, no-talking zone for the serious reader/writer
  • "Afternoon" Tea and Scones (with clotted cream, jam, lemon curd, and butter--all day!)
  • "The Library"-- The Bricks and Scones library is based on a swap-in/swap-out system. You may read as many books as you'd like during your visit. If you wish to borrow or take a book for keeps, you must contribute a book of your own in return. Feel free to donate to the collection!
  • "The Board Games"--Tucked away beneath the community board are the board games! (Please do not take games from the premises).
NOTE: The Study is a no-talking room dedicated to quiet reading and writing. Each person gets his or her own desk and lamp (if you're lucky, you can nab the corner desk by the window with a great street view!). Come take a look for yourself (and get some work done while you're at it)!
