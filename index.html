Coffee, Tea, & Good Eats

BRICKS & SCONES

We are a family-run coffee shop with a focus on providing a comfortable and relaxing space for the local community. Scrumptious pastries are baked on-site every day, and we serve a variety of sandwiches, salads, and breakfast foods. We offer the highest-quality coffee and a wide selection of teas from Lupicia Fresh Tea. With plenty of indoor and outdoor seating (and free wi-fi), we hope to serve as a "third place" for those who want to get out of the house or relax during their time away from work.
